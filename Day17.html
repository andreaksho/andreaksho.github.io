Day 17 - Triangular Lines

Another day to practice patterns.

This was a lot harder than I thought. As you can probably tell, I gave up half way of doing patterned triangles. I wasn't able to distrubute the lines evenly diagonally. I thought I had it when I distributed them horizontally, then vertically. Then when I was trying to do it again, I couldn't. All the lines would just clump together. So, in order to get the lines to distrbute evenly, I had do it manually, which was very, very time consuming.

There must be a easier/better/efficient way to solve this. Because of the time constraint, I wasn't able to solve this problem.
