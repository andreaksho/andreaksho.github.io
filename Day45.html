Day 45 - Recording...

This was a pretty straightforward design with simple shapes. Today, I decided to copy my recording device in my phone. It has a simple white background with black lettering. However, just like my previous design, I wanted to change it up.

I completely changed the background colour to black and instead of filling in the shapes, I only kept the outline of them. I wanted to go for a blue and black theme again, but then since I did that yesterday, I changed it up by using purple this time.

The calendar and the phone logos were not created by me, but everything else was. The basic shapes and lines are getting easier and easier everyday, taking less time every time. :)
