Day 33 - Are You Responsive?

Today I decided to place my responsive wireframes that I've been working for a couple weeks now into their respective templates.

In order to get the templates I wanted, I downloaded a file where it included a bunch of different templates online. Even though it's not as detailed as I wanted them to be, but this will do. Then for a more creative look, I added shadows for all of the layouts, then added a gradient background.

As you can tell, the website is still in the works. The websites are only wireframes, they are not fully developed yet, but I will continue to work on them until they can be released. The main purpose of this was to see how my responsive wireframes look on a template.
