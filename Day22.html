Day 22 - What A Beauty

This was a design I've always wanted to create: Simplying something complicated.

The hardest part about this one was to ignore all the details and focus on the bigger picture. I tried looking for simple graphics of Belle to help me with this, but there weren't any. In the end, I realized I put too much detail in shadows. Also, not putting layers in her hair was a struggle.

All in all, pretty satisfied with this design. :)
