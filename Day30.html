Day 30 - Stopwatch

Final day for this app. This was a really cool app to create.

My key takeaways from creating this app includes:

- Use of the gradient tool on lines: For cohesiveness, the lines needed to have a gradient effect on them to match well with the background gradient effect.

- Creation of simple but effective icons: I used simple shapes ranging from rectangles, triangles and circles to create symbols that are easily understood.

- Colour sychronization: In order to have the app/page to look as a whole, colour must match well with the rest of the elements on the page. Everyday, I had a different colour for each page, and I had to find the right colours for each specific element to make the whole page as one unit, than a page with different units.
