Day 2 - Heyo!

Today I added a bit of a character to this potato head.

Not a big fan of what came out of it. From this, I realized I still need a ton practice, but I like where I'm going with this. I'm still in the realms of a steep learning curve right now, so my ability to perfect the black lines was a struggle. After creating the not-so-perfect expression, I had trouble whether to outline the body or not. This was a fun exercise overall, but with the time constraint, I had to move on.

PRO: I figured what I need to work on.

CON: This still needs a lot of work.
