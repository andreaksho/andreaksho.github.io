Day 32 - TipCalc

I changed the UI of a friend's current TipCalc mobile app.

To be honest, I don't believe this app has enough content to entice users to download it. But this re-design was not to change the content, but to re-create it. I changed the colour scheme from green and white to red and black. The whole app only has two pages in total, so I decided to design both the pages.

I also installed a iphone template border to show how the app looks on mobile. As for the background, I used a grid fill at 200%, then added a red and black gradient on top.
