Day 1 - I'm Back

Today is my first day of starting this challenge.

To be honest, I've made a couple logos here and there, but I stopped because life/school/work got in the way. However after starting a User Experience online course at Designlab, I decided to challenge myself with this A Design A Day to improve my design skills. I have basic knowledge of Photoshop and Illustrator before, but this time I want to learn something different by using Sketch for all the designs.

I wasn't sure of what to design first, so I decided to start with basic shapes. Then this potato head became an idea that I placed on an artboard.
