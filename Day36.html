Day 36 - What Are Your Skills?

Another fun UI to create.

I used the user icons I created at the beginning of my #AndreaDesigns journey as actually users. I took my current skill sets and created skills bar. This page allows users to see other users' skillsets, as well as their experience and contact information located in the other two tabs.

To make the profile a little more personal, I changed the background and tabs colours according to the user's profile. I also added relevant pages a user may want to add under the job title for visitors to see, such as Facebook, Github, LinkedIn, etc.

To clarify, this page is seen by a visitor, and not the user itself. If it's the user itself, there would be edit icons, which will allow the user to personalize their profile. This includes changing the profil picture, adding/removing more social pages, adding/removing skillsets, and more.
