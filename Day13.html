Day 13 - Circles on Circles

Today was for learning. I've been seeing a lot of images on Instagram where they create images using a circle grid. So I decided to try it out today.

I followed this Step-By-Step to create this image. I still don't fullly understand the idea because when I subtracted the circle, it gave me something completely opposite.
