Day 31 - 1+1=?

At first, this calculator app was very tedious. I had to continuously change the sizes and fonts of every circle/text on the app so it looked consistent throughout.

It was after the creation of the app when the fun began. I didn't want to have another image that's just flat on a square artboard. I've seen designs where the mobile would be lying flat on an angle. So I decided to try it out.

First, I had to transform the image in a way where it'd make sense. It took some trial and error at first, but after a couple of tries, I was able to get the angle I wanted it to be in. Next, I would need to rotate the transformed image. After positioning the image the way I wanted it, I decided to create a shadow.So, I duplicated the image and moved it down a bit. I filled the colour in with grey. Then came the challenging part. The shadow was too sharp. I had to look for a tool that would blur the shadow. Luckily, I was able to find the correct tool: Motion Blur @ 50px. I was able to create the effect I wanted with this tool.

So happy with the shadow outcome because I was able to learned something I've been wanting to try out for a long time. :)
