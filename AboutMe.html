HELLO THERE!

I'm currently in my fourth year at the UBC Sauder School of Business with a specialization in Marketing. I am so grateful to have experienced both Marketing and Accounting opportunities in the past couple of years. However, I didn't feel like I was giving 100% all the time - something was missing and I didn't know what it was.

It was at the beginning of 2016 when the term "User Experience Design" was introduced to me. Being an artist, loving to understand how people behave, and driven by constant improvement in all things, I began to submerge myself into all kinds of online articles, random UX blogs, local workshops, and even in some basic coding to create my own website.

As I dug more into this topic, my interest grew along with it. Everyday I would set aside a couple hours just to read up about it. At the beginning of 2017, I decided to purchase a notebook dedicated to everything that was related to design. This was just the beginning of a steep learning curve in my journey in UX. In early February, I enrolled into the UX Academy course at Designlab. This course has taught me a lot through various projects, starting from research all the way to testing (and continuously improving them along the way). Through this program, I have had the opportunity to meet both local and global aspiring UX designers and mentors to guide me through.

I'm so glad I've decided to explore this route. What I've learned is to not only completely engage in things you want to do, but also fully commit to it.

Come follow me on my A Design A Day journey either on this website or on Instagram, as I continuously challenge myself everyday and hopefully even inspire you to challenge yourself one day.
